Why Choose Us

Dimate Inc. advises U.S. and Russian companies and organizations seeking cooperative ventures and markets in Russia, Eastern Europe, and North America. Based in Rochester, NY, the company was established in 1997 to facilitate U.S.-Russian business contacts and establish U.S.-based brands in Russia and the Commonwealth of Independent States. We also assist companies from Russia and other nations of Eastern Europe in finding markets or business partners in the United States.

Since 1997, Dimate Inc. has accomplished:


  1. GROWING BUSINESS - Helped launch a chain of photographic mini-labs in three Russian regional cities.​
  2. MEDIA RELATIONS - Coordinated corporate and media relations for a major European marketing research company in Eastern Europe.​
  3. CONSULTING - Coordinated merger negotiations between leading U.S. pollster and top Russian marketing research agency.
  4. EXPANDING HORIZONS - Set up a manufacturing and distribution operation in Moscow for leading U.S. producer of durable imaging technologies.​
  5. INTEGRATION - Grown markets for nearly a dozen American products in Russia.
Whether it's troubleshooting for ongoing projects or a startup operation, the team at Dimate Inc. has the resources and resourcefulness to solve problems and move your projects toward measurable results.
